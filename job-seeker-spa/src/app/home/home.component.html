Job-Seeker Bay Info

The job-seekers bay is a software solution for recruiting talented and skillful candidates in various sectors. It’s a multi-user system which allows job-seekers, employees and admins to operate by logging-in themselves with appropriate user credentials. Majorly, it provides two tasks: one would assist job-seekers to upload their CV and find their relevant jobs and the other, would allow employees and admins to maintain and monitor the job-seekers, jobs and other related information.
